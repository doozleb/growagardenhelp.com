Pets

This page provides detailed information on pets in Grow A Garden, including their abilities, bonuses, and how to use them to optimize your garden. Use this as a reference to understand the impact of each pet and how to unlock or upgrade them in the game.

Pet Abilities and Bonuses

Each pet provides unique bonuses to your garden, such as increased growth speed, higher mutation chances, or special event triggers. Combine different pets to maximize your garden's efficiency and rewards.

  • Growth Speed: Pets that help your crops grow faster.
  • Mutation Chance: Pets that increase the chance of rare mutations.
  • Event Bonuses: Pets that trigger or enhance special events.
  • Other Effects: Unique abilities specific to certain pets.
