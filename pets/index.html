Pets

This page provides detailed information on pets in Grow A Garden, including their abilities, bonuses, and how to use them to optimize your garden. Use this as a reference to understand the impact of each pet and how to unlock or upgrade them in the game.

Pet Abilities and Bonuses

Each pet provides unique bonuses to your garden, such as increased growth speed, higher mutation chances, or special event triggers. Combine different pets to maximize your garden's efficiency and rewards.

  • Growth Speed: Pets that help your crops grow faster.
  • Mutation Chance: Pets that increase the chance of rare mutations.
  • Event Bonuses: Pets that trigger or enhance special events.
  • Other Effects: Unique abilities specific to certain pets.

Pet List

Pet Name Trait / Ability / Effect
BunnyEats a carrot (normal or chocolate variant) and increases its selling value by 1.53x or more when it ages.
DogTries to dig up a seed from your garden (Seed Shop ones) every minute. The seed isn't guaranteed, though, with a success rate of 5.14%.
Golden LabWorks like the Dog but has a better shot at finding a seed, with a success rate of about 10.31%.
BeeGoes to a random fruit or veggie every 24 minutes and 45 seconds and makes it Pollinated.
Black BunnyRaises the price of carrots (or chocolate carrots) by 1.53 times when consumed, doing it just a bit sooner than a regular bunny.
CatNaps for 10.12 seconds every 1 minute and 20 seconds, making nearby fruits (within 10.12 studs) grow 1.25x bigger.
ChickenIncreases egg hatch speed by 10.36%.
DeerHas a 2.53% chance of making berry fruits stay on the plant even after harvesting. Berry fruits include Celestiberry, Strawberry, Blueberry, Cranberry, and Raspberry.
HedgehogMakes prickly fruits 1.52 times larger. In Grow a Garden, prickly fruits include Cactus, Durian, Dragon Fruit, and Pineapple.
KiwiFinds and sits on the egg with the highest hatch time every minute, reducing its hatch time by 20.24%.
MonkeyRefunds fruits when selling (only 2.55% likelihood). Rare fruits like Beanstalk, Candy Blossom, Moon Mango, and more have a small chance of a return.
Orange TabbyA better version of the cat. It sleeps for about 1.30 minutes and makes fruits within 15.16 studs become larger by 1.51% during that time.
PigHas a chance to turn nearby fruits within 15 studs into mutated variants. The likelihood of this occurring is just 2.02%, and the ability lasts for only 15.35 seconds every two minutes.
RoosterRaises egg hatch speed by 20.93%.
Spotted DeerLike the deer's trait, but boosts the chance of berry fruits remaining on the plant after picking to around 5.10%.
Honey BeeTriggers the Pollinated mutation on plants every 19.43 minutes.
Blood HedgehogMakes prickly fruits 2.02% bigger, and any mutated ones among them sell for 1.16% more.
Blood KiwiIncreases both the hatch time and hatch speed of eggs in your garden by 45.56% and 20.25%, respectively.
CowSpeeds up the growth of all plants within 8.18 studs by 1.12%, making their fruits spawn faster than usual.
FrogMakes a random crop grow faster for a full 24 hours, meaning its fruits ripen quicker. This ability activates every 19.48 minutes.
MoleAround every 1.20 minutes, it digs up a gear or Sheckles for you. Gear includes a Trowel, Lightning Rod, Watering Can, all Sprinklers, and anything from the Gear Shop.
Moon CatSnoozes for 20.18 seconds every minute and enlarges fruits by 1.51x within a small range. It also helps Moon-type fruits (Moon Mango, Moon Blossom, Moonflower, etc.) stay on the plant after picking.
PandaJumps over and eats bamboo, increasing its selling value by 1.57x.
Polar BearMay grant a Chilled or Frozen mutation to random fruits every 1.29 minutes, though the likelihood is just 10.14%.
Sea OtterActs like a living watering can. It sprays water on random plants every 15 seconds, helping their fruits grow faster.
Silver MonkeyCan return your fruits and veggies after selling them, but the probability is only 7.76%.
TurtleHelps sprinklers (Basic, Advanced, Godly, Master, and Chocolate) last 20.47% longer.
WaspTurns a random fruit Pollinated every 29.46 minutes, then bites and raises an active pet's cooldown by almost a minute every 9.58 minutes.
Tarantula HawkWorks like a Wasp, but it's quicker. This hawk can mutate a plant in 24.39 minutes and improves cooldown by 82.01 seconds.
Petal BeeNot only does it make your flower plants not disappear after picking (1.13% chance), but it also gives a crop the Pollinated mutation after a round of 24.49 minutes.
MothFills the Hunger Bar of an active pet completely every 12.55 minutes.
Brown MouseAllows players to jump higher and also gain 803.74 EXP every eight minutes, to age pretty fast.
CaterpillarLeafy plants within its range produce fruits faster. Leafy plants are those with green leaves, such as Beanstalk, Grape, Eggplant, Pepper, etc.
Echo FrogNearly every 15 minutes, one of your crops will experience faster growth for an entire real-world day. The crop will keep giving fruits more rapidly than usual.
Giant AntWhen harvesting crops, there's a 10.38% possibility that they'll be cloned, giving you two fruits instead of one. Although rare plants are less likely to duplicate, candy fruits are more likely.
Grey MouseGives the Mouse 521.75 EXP every 10 minutes and boosts players' jump height.
OwlAll walking critters in your garden gain 0.23 EXP every second. This is helpful in aging them and then selling them to unlock extra pet slots.
Praying MantisPrays for 10.30 seconds every 1.32 minutes, increasing your chance of finding a mutated fruit on its radar. You can get any normal mutation except event ones or those triggered by devs (i.e., Plasma and Dawnbound).
Red FoxHops into your neighbor's garden every 8.57 minutes and tries to grab a seed from any of their non-event plants to bring you. Red Foxes have quite a low success rate if the garden has high-rarity seeds.
Red Giant AntWith this animal, there's a 5.16% chance that picking normal fruits (especially berries) gives you an extra one in your inventory.
SnailYou might get a seed from the crop you just harvested (5.08% likelihood).
SquirrelWhen planting a seed, it might return to your inventory. This means it'll grow the crop for you, but you'll also get the seed back.
Chicken ZombieYou might get a Zombified fruit every 30 seconds (with only a 20.30% chance). Egg hatch speed also increases by 10.15%.
FireflyGives you a Shocked veggie or fruit every 1.32 minutes (3.07% success rate).
Bear BeeGrants a nearby fruit, the HoneyGlazed mutation, every 25 minutes.
ButterflyFinds a plant with five or more mutations every 29.56 minutes and replaces all existing variants with the Rainbow mutation. She doesn't touch favorited plants.
Blood OwlAll pets roaming your farm earn an extra 0.53 EXP on top of what they get from being fed.
DragonflyCan turn a random fruit in your garden gold every 4.5 minutes.
Night OwlGrants 0.23 EXP to all active critters.
RaccoonHangs out in your neighbor's garden every 15 minutes and duplicates a fruit they have for you (even event exclusives).
Queen BeeCompletely refreshes an active pet's trait cooldown every 29.37 minutes and Pollinates one of your garden's plants about every 24.37 minutes.
Disco BeeThe first-ever pet that can give your plants the Disco mutation (only a 12.28% chance) every 19.49 minutes
StarfishWanders in the garden and gains 6.36 experience points, making it grow older. Best for opening pet slots.
FlamingoIncreases crop growth speed (both seeds and fully grown) for about 15 minutes by standing on a leg every 3.57 minutes. Its ability only affects crops within a range of 13.45 studs.
CrabWalks up to another player on the server every 6.12 minutes and pinches them to snag around 260 to 460 Sheckles. They don't lose any money.
SeagullGives you a free seed of the plant you just shoveled (flowers and veggies only), though the chance is just 3.53%. The seagull doesn't work on fruit crops like Mango, Moon Mango, Pineapple, Banana, and others.
ToucanTurns your tropical plants bigger and gives them a mutation within a 25.77-stud range. The chances are 1.70x for size and 1.18x for mutation. In Grow a Garden, Tropical plants include Coconut, Mango, Dragon Fruit, Watermelon, Cacao, and more.
Sea TurtleWaters a fruit every 1.97 minutes, giving it a 10.10% chance of transforming into a Wet variant (2x value). Also helps age a random pet every 10.52 minutes, granting it 1,004.77 EXP.
SealWhen you sell a pet, there's a 2.24% chance you'll get its egg back in your inventory. For example, selling a Mouse might give you a Mythical Egg.
OrangutanUseful for crafting sprays, seeds, sprinklers, and other tools, as it gives you a small chance to get some crafting materials back in your backpack once activated.
OstrichGives newly hatched critters an age bonus, starting them anywhere between 1 and 4.49.
PeacockFans its beautiful feathers to reduce the cooldown of pets hanging around it by 60.78 seconds every 9.58 minutes.
CapybaraNearby pets (within 15.65 studs) won't lose hunger and build up 3.65 experience points every second.
Mimic OctopusCopies the ability of an active pet every 20 minutes.
Scarlet MacawHas a 15.95% chance to mutate your fruit into a Verdant variant (4x value) every 11.50 minutes.
MeerkatEvery 10 minutes, reduces the cooldown of a random pet by 21.92 seconds. 10.96% chance of doing it a second time.
Sand Snake2.24% chance of duplicating any seed or gear brought from shops.
Axolotl6.66% chance of summer plants remaining after collection.
Hyacinth MacawEvery 8.26 minutes, has a 16.78% chance of applying Wiltproof mutation to fruits.
Fennec FoxEvery 22.03 minutes, copies random mutation from other player's fruit and applies to one of your fruits which lacks that mutation.
